About me

Hello there!

My name is Alexandre, aka NotAlex.

I started playing Star Wars Unlimited one week after its release. Like many people my age, I'm a Star Wars fan.

I used to play a lot of board games - much less since Star Wars Unlimited entered my life!

I'm a software engineer, and I love building stuff. This website is an attempt to solve THE question many SWU players wonder about: will my deck beat Jango?!?
