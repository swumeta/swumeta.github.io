This page features a curated selection of Star Wars Unlimited tournaments that have made a significant impact on the competitive metagame. To maintain quality and relevance, we only include events with at least 64 participants. This approach ensures that the tournament data represented here reflects substantial competitive environments where meaningful strategies and deck trends can be observed.

While we strive to include all major tournaments, this listing is not exhaustive. If you know of a qualifying (with 64+ players) that isn't featured here, please contact us with the tournament details, and we'll consider adding it to our database.
